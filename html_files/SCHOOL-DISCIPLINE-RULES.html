Rules and regulations

  • Be punctual, report to school and class on time. School begins at 8:00 a.m. If a student arrives in school after 8:00 he/she needs to report to the office to receive an admit/tardy slip.
  • Respect each other at all times. Disagreements will be settled by discussing the problem and seeking a solution that is fair to all concerned.
  • Mobile phones in school are forbidden
  • Avoid using abusive or profane language and gestures.
  • Respect Senior Citizens.
  • Adhere to the rules of school.
  • Breaking the school rules is a crime. For this you May be out of school for a day.
  • Students should treat school staff with respect at all times and be courteous to fellow students.
  • For safety and security, parents should refrain from sending money with their child without specific written notice from the school.
