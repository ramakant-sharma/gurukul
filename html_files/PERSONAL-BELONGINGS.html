PERSONAL-BELONGINGS

  • All students are requested to not forget to bring their books, pen, pencils and all those things which are necessary for the school.
  • The tie and belt in the school uniform are compulsory.
  • Every morning after the prayer, the school uniform will be examined.
  • Every day the oath will be administered in the school, and the student will be motivated to do good work.
  • A home-like atmosphere is kept in the school. For this reason children come to school without fear.
  • Children's nails will be examined every Monday and Wednesday.
